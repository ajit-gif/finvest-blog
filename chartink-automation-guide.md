# Chartink Automation: Complete Guide to Automated Stock Screening and Trading Alerts in 2026

## What Is Chartink Automation?

Chartink automation is the process of automatically capturing stock scan results from Chartink and delivering them to external platforms such as Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, Google Sheets, broker APIs, and custom trading dashboards.

Instead of manually checking scans throughout the day, traders can receive real-time alerts whenever their conditions are met.

## How Chartink Automation Works

1. A Chartink scan identifies a stock matching predefined criteria.
2. An alert is generated.
3. The alert is sent to an automation workflow.
4. The workflow processes the signal.
5. Notifications or trading actions are triggered.

This reduces reaction time and improves execution efficiency.

## Can Chartink Automation Place Trades Automatically?

Yes. By connecting Chartink alerts to broker APIs, traders can create partially or fully automated workflows.

Typical flow:

Chartink → Alert Engine → Broker API → Order Placement

However, risk management and position sizing should always be part of the workflow.

## Best Practices

- Avoid alert overload
- Test strategies before live deployment
- Maintain proper risk controls
- Monitor automation health regularly
