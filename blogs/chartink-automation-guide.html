Chartink Automation Guide

Complete guide to automate Chartink scans, Telegram alerts and trading workflows.

What is Chartink Automation?

Chartink automation allows traders to automatically receive stock scan alerts and connect them with Telegram, dashboards and broker APIs.

Benefits

  • Real-time alerts
  • Faster execution
  • Multi-strategy monitoring
  • Reduced manual work

Use Cases

  • Breakout scanners
  • Volume alerts
  • Momentum trading
  • Swing trading setups

Conclusion

Chartink automation helps traders build scalable and efficient trading workflows.
